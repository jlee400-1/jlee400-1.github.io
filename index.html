ABOUT

I’m a graduate student at Pratt Institute, pursuing an M.S. in Museums and Digital Culture. With a focus on visitor experiences, I aim to enhance visitor engagement by integrating technology into cultural environments through interactive, visitor-centered installations.

Based in New York City, I’m always open to connecting and discussing ideas related to museum work and digital innovation. I’m actively seeking opportunities in museums and other cultural organizations.

RESEARCH HIGHLIGHT

My research journey spans virtual exhibition development and curatorial practice. As a Research Assistant at Academia Sinica (Taiwan), I systematically reviewed user studies in virtual exhibitions, aiming to enhance visitor engagement and shape new theoretical frameworks. In addition, I curated an online exhibition focusing on Taiwanese cultural history, blending academic collaboration with digital interpretation. For more details, please visit this page.
